On-Page SEO

On-page SEO focuses on the parts of a webpage that can be edited directly. This includes the text, headings, links, images, page titles, and other content that appears on the page.

Main Elements

A page should be organized in a way that is easy to read. Headings should describe the content clearly, links should be useful, and the page should give visitors the information they expected to find.
